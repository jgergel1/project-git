Gasparilla Half Marathon

If staying active is more your speed than maybe join the Gasparilla half marathon. Every year people from all over come to run in their regular old running gear but if you prefer a little more excitement dress like a pirate! Yes, I said pirate. Gasparilla celebrates the time when Tampa was invaded by pirates many moons ago. It is a great event full of families and lots of fun.

Camel Kingdom

Grab some of your favorite and delicious fair food and proceed on to the FREE camel drive-thru to hang out with these beautiful camels! Get up close, take photos and learn about these incredible, rare Snow White camels and the never before seen blue-eyed spotted camels! You can see first hand how well these camels are cared for!
