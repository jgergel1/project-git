About Us

Living in Florida is great. It means beautiful, sunny weather pretty much year round. It does get hot but we’ve got plenty of ways to cool off so we don’t mind. Because we live here we don’t necessarily want to go visit the same beaches full of college kids looking for some fun. We look for beaches and other attractions that may be less known but are just as beautiful or entertaining as any other. That’s what we’re about here. We focus on the beaches, food, and entertainment that Florida has to offer that will not include a set of mouse ears.
